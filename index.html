Short Bio

Xiangchun Chen is a PhD candidate in Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University , supervised by Prof. Cao Jiannong. Previously, he received the Bachelor Degree of Computer Science and Technology from Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology in 2021.

His research interests lie in AI Agents and Large Language Models (LLMs). His current work focuses on building autonomous AI agents for complex scheduling and resource management. Previously, his research also covered digital twin, reinforcement learning, and edge computing.
